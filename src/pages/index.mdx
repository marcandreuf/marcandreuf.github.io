### Work:
Marc is an independent full-stack software engineer, currently based in South Korea, with over 25 years of experience building digital products and automation systems for business growth.

He focuses on building custom SaaS prototypes, AI automations, integrations, and web apps that solve real workflow bottlenecks and create measurable efficiency gains.

Software quality and test automation remain part of his toolkit, now integrated as a scaling layer after product delivery to ensure reliability and long-term maintainability.

He has a knack for shipping quickly—from product planning and architecture to production delivery—and shares practical learnings through blog posts so others can build faster.
