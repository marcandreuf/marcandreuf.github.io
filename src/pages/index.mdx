### Work: 
Marc is a freelance and full-stack developer in South Korea who is passionate about building Web Applications, AI automation and QA consulting. He has a knack for all things launching solutions, from planning and designing to crafting business needs with code. He loves hanging out with his family, playing football, and reading when he is not online. Currently, he is living off his development services, which are called to build web apps and test automated frameworks.
