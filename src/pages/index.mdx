### Work: 
Marc is an independent full-stack software engineer based in South Korea with over 20 years of work experience. He is passionate about building Automated solutions to solve business problems. Marc specialises in finding the best cost-effective automated solutions leveraging AI and Web frameworks while also providing impactful Software Quality consulting for entire development teams. 

He has a knack for launching solutions, from planning and designing to crafting business needs with code. When he is not online, Marc loves hanging out with his family, playing football, and reading. 

Currently, he lives off his development services, which include building complex AI automation, Systems integrations, APIs, progressive web/web apps, and testing automated frameworks. 

He publishes blog posts to share everything he learns so others can reuse the code and solve problems faster.
